Problem

Numerous beauty and wellness businesses face an issue with the large number of people who are not attending their appointments.
That leads to vacant slots of time that in turn lead to profit loss. On the other end, the clients do not have an easy method of determining the service providers in a certain area and there is no reliable way of looking at feedback or reviews from other clients.

Solution

PlanInsta is a tool for both beauty and wellness businesses and clients that makes the process of handling appointments reliable and fast, by using technology. Businesses are able to promote their services within the application and become more selective as they have access to customer ratings. Clients are able to browse through a range of rated and reviewed service providers, make an appointment and get confirmations faster and easier than traditional approaches.

Customer segments

The platform will be used by 2 categories of users.

On one hand, our app will serve the beauty and wellness companies that want to minimize the costs of managing appointments.

On the other hand, the platform will handle the potential customers of those companies. They will be able to easily make an appointment that best suits their needs.
