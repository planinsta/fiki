Interviews

For the face-to-face interviews we decided to address 5 basic questions to the representatives of beauty and wellness companies and 6 basic questions to their clients.
The questions were the following:

Customers

  • Personal information: age, ocupation etc.
  • For what services do you usually make an appointment at a beauty and wellness salon?
  • Do you always go to the same beauty and wellness salon? Argument the answer.
  • What method do you use to make an appointment and why?
  • What difficulties are you facing during yout interaction with the beuaty and wellness salons?
  • How do you prefer to pay for a service that you have benefited from after a visit to a beauty and wellness salon? (cash, card)

Beauty and wellness companies

  • How many employees has the salon you are employed in / the salon you own?
  • How do you keep track of customers' appointments?
  • Do you noticed problems in the management of the appointments? Argument the answer.
  • What are the difficulties you face in managing your business and interacting with clients?
  • What is the percentage of clients who did not honor their appointments?
